Queso Fundido

Description

A cheesy dip filled with flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 poblano chile
  • 2 teaspoons vegetable oil
  • 4 ounces fresh chorizo, pulled out of the casing
  • 2 teaspoons hot sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon powdered adobo seasoning
  • 6 ounces shredded Chihuahua cheese
  • 2 scallions, chopped
  • 1/2 cup cilantro, chopped
  • Limes and tortillas for serving

Directions

  1. Roast chile on the stove or a grill
  2. Roughly chop after removing peel, stem, and seeds
  3. Cook chorizo in oil until browned and cooked through
  4. Turn on broiler
  5. Layer in a cast iron skillet:
    • half of chile
    • 1 tsp hot sauce
    • 1/4 tsp adobo seasoning
    • half of cheese
  6. Place in broiler and cook until cheese is starting to brown
  7. Remove from oven and repeat layers with remaining ingredients
  8. Broil until top begins to brown again
  9. Top with scallion and cilantro, and serve with limes and tortillas
