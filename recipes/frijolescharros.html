Frijoles Charros

Description

A long soak makes the beans cook quickly.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound dried pinto beans
  • 6 cups chicken stock
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 12 ounces bacon, diced
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 jalepeno pepper, minced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cans fire roasted tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup cilantro, chopped

Directions

  1. Cover beans with water and soak overnight
  2. Drain and rinse beans, adding to a large dutch oven
  3. Add stock and bay leaves, bring to a boil then simmer for 45 minutes
  4. Cook bacon until starting to brown, then add onions and chiles
  5. Add garlic until just fragrant, then add tomatoes
  6. Add bacon and tomato mixture to dutch oven
  7. Cook until beans are creamy and liquid has thickened
  8. Serve with cilantro
