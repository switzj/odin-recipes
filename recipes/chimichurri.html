Chimichurri

Description

A sauce made of fresh herbs that is great with grilled meat.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup fresh parsley
  • 5 medium cloves garlic
  • 2 tablespoons fresh oregano
  • 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes

Directions

  1. In a food processor, add parsley, garlic, and oregano
  2. Pulse until finely chopped
  3. Transfer to a bowl and add oil, vinegar, salt, and red pepper flakes
  4. Whisk to combine thoroughly and store in the refrigerator for up to two days
